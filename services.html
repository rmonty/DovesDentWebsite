Our Services

Hail Damage Repair

When a storm rolls through and leaves your vehicle looking like a golf ball, Doves Dent is who you call. We specialize in high-volume hail restoration using precision PDR techniques that bring every panel back to factory shape.

Because we work directly with all major insurance carriers, most customers only pay their deductible. We handle the paperwork, the adjuster coordination, and the repair — you just drop off your vehicle.

  • Full-vehicle hail assessment with photo documentation
  • Direct insurance billing and claim coordination
  • No paint, no filler — factory finish preserved
  • Lifetime warranty on all hail repairs
  • Same-day or next-day turnaround for most vehicles

Paintless Dent Repair

Door dings, shopping cart dents, minor creases from parking lots — these small imperfections are exactly what PDR was designed to fix. Using specialized lighting and precision tools, we massage the metal back to its original shape from behind the panel.

The result? A perfect repair with your original factory paint completely intact. No primer, no color matching, no risk of mismatched paint years down the road.

  • Door dings and parking lot impacts
  • Minor creases and panel dents
  • Pre-sale vehicle reconditioning
  • Fleet and dealership dent removal
  • Factory paint fully preserved
